Alzheimer's

Alzheimer's is a degenerative disease of the brain, most common among people aged 65 and over. The disease causes a gradual worsening of brain functions and unfortunately to this day the reasons for its development are unknown and a cure has not yet been found. However, #early diagnosis of the disease may delay its worsening thanks to appropriate drugs.
